Creative Media Studio

1. Rooms are available on a first-come, first-served basis. Booking can be made in 7 days advance through the Library Room Booking System. 
2. The Studio can be reserved by eligible users. 
3. The Studio can be reserved and occupied by a minimum of one user. 
4. Duration of each booking session is 30-minute within Library Opening Hours.
5. A maximum of 4 sessions or 2 hours can be reserved per day. 
6. A maximum of 2 bookings can be made per week (from Sunday to Saturday) per eligible user. 
7. Requesters who failed to check-in to the reserved room 10 minutes into the booking session will be forfeiting their reservation. 
8. No-show without making any cancellation will count against the quota limit. 
9. Users must make sure they have saved their media files in their own storage devices before leaving the Studio. 
10. Eating or drinking is strictly prohibited in the room. 
11. Users are responsible for any damages of the furniture and equipment in the Studio.
